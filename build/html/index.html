Welcome to the Python e-textbook!¶

Welcome to this interactive e-textbook on Python! This book will guide you through the basics of loops, how to control iterations, and how to solve common problems using loops in Python. Here’s what you’ll be learning:

Table of Contents

This e-textbook consists of two main modules designed to introduce you to while loops and their applications in Python:

  1. Introduction to Loops Learn the basic structure of loops and their types, including count-controlled and event-controlled loops.

  2. More About While Loops Explore the details of while loops, including how to nest loops, work with conditions, and apply them in more advanced scenarios with practical examples.

How to Use This E-textbook¶

  • Interactive Exercises: Each chapter contains code snippets and multiple-choice questions. To submit your answer, simply click the ‘Check’ button after selecting your response.

  • XP Points: Earn XP points by solving the problems. Each question has an associated point value, and you’ll be awarded points for correct answers. If you don’t get the correct answer on your first try, you have a second attempt. The number of points available for each question is shown in brackets next to the question.

Tip: You can track your total XP on the top-right corner of the screen.

  • Badges: Badges are awarded based on your performance. Each module offers two types of badges:

    • The Module Completion Badge, earned by answering all questions correctly and marking the module as complete.

    • The Expert Achiever Badge, awarded for answering all questions correctly on your first attempt.

Tip: You can see your earned badges on the top-right corner of the screen as well.

Make sure to answer each question carefully to maximize your XP and earn badges! Ready to get started? Click on ‘Introduction to loops’ below.
